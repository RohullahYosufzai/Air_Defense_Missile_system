FLAWLESS PERFORMANCE BY BAYRAKTAR TB3 UCAV IN THE HARSH CONDITIONS OF THE BALTIC SEA

During NATO's Steadfast Dart-2026 exercise, the Bayraktar TB3 UCAV, deployed aboard TCG ANADOLU, successfully completed its flight demonstration over the Baltic Sea despite freezing cold, heavy snowfall, and severe winds. As the only aircraft capable of flying in the harsh weather conditions during this phase of the exercise, the Bayraktar TB3 received full marks from allied nations' representatives.

18 February 2026
BAYRAKTAR TB3 UCAV MAKES A SHOW OF FORCE AT NATO EXERCISE

The Bayraktar TB3 UCAV took to the world stage as part of NATO's Steadfast Dart 2026 exercise. Taking off autonomously from TCG ANADOLU's short runway, Bayraktar TB3 scored direct hits on surface targets with a dual salvo MAM-L strike during a live-fire exercise in the Baltic Sea, successfully completing its first operational demonstration abroad.

NEW DOCUMENTARY TELLS THE LIFE STORY OF ÖZDEMIR BAYRAKTAR

The inspirational life story of Baykar founder Özdemir Bayraktar has been brought to the screen in a new documentary entitled “Özdemir Bayraktar | An Akıncı Passed Through This World.” The documentary, featuring eyewitness accounts and previously unseen archival footage, is now streaming on Baykar’s YouTube channel and various digital platforms. A comprehensive book prepared as part of the same project has also been published simultaneously.
